Mac and Cheese Pasta

üïõ Prep Time: 15 mins | üç≥ Cook Time: 25 mins | üçΩ Serves: 4

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 2 slice Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bread crumbs
  1. Boil macaroni and drain and set aside.
  2. Saucepan melt butter over medium heat.Stir in flour to form a roux.
  3. Gradually add milk while stirring. Cook until thickened.
  4. Stir in cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce. Mix well.
  6. Optional: Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and broil until golden.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
